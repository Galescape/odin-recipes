Vegetarian Lasagna

This is a recipe for a delicious vegetable lasagna for people who will cut down on meat!

This healthy lasagna both tastes good and is easy to make! Takes about 60min to make.

You will need the following ingredients:

  • 3 Carrots
  • 2 yellow onions
  • 1 zucchini
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • 2 cups/480 ml crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup/230 ml half and half
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup/30 g of fresh basil
  • 6 cups/250 g baby spinach
  • 16 pcs lasagna noodles
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 5.3 oz/150 g shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups/ 80 g mixed salad

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 440°F / 225°C.
  2. Peel and shred the carrots, slice the xzucchini.
  3. Chop the yeallow onions and garlic.
  4. Add oil to a frying pan and fry the onion, garlic, carrots and zucchini on high heat until soft.
  5. Add crushed tomatoes, half and half, chili flakes, salt and pepper.
  6. Let simmer for about 10-12 min on medium heat.
  7. Grease a lasagna dish with oil and start assembling.
  8. Start with the tomato mixture, then layer the noodles and spinach.
  9. Repeat and sprinkle the shredded cheese on top along with basil leaves.
  10. Bake in the oven for 30 min, serve with mixed salad.
  11. Enjoy!
